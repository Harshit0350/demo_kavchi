F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of batteries does Kavchi manufacture?

We specialize in high-performance lithium-ion batteries for e-scooters, e-rickshaws, e-cycles, and home inverters.

Why choose lithium-ion batteries over traditional lead-acid batteries?

Lithium-ion batteries are lighter, charge faster, have a longer lifespan, and are more energy-efficient than lead-acid alternatives.

How long does a Kavchi battery last?

Our batteries typically last up to 1200+ charge cycles, depending on usage and maintenance—providing several years of reliable service.

How long does it take to fully charge a Kavchi battery?

Charging time varies by capacity, but our batteries are equipped with fast-charging technology, reducing full charge time to as low as 4 to 6 hours.

Do Kavchi batteries come with a warranty?

Yes, all Kavchi batteries come with a standard warranty ranging from 1 to 3 years, depending on the product.

Are the batteries safe from overheating or overcharging?

Absolutely. Our advanced Battery Management System (BMS) provides protection from overcharging, overheating, short circuits, and deep discharge.
